# Module 1: The Rewire (Identity Shift)

**Theme:** Stop coding. Start leading.

---

This module covers the fundamental identity shift required when transitioning from Senior Engineer to Engineering Manager. You'll learn to let go of individual contribution and embrace your new role as a force multiplier.

## What You'll Learn

### 1.1 The Maker vs. Multiplier Metric
Break your addiction to the "Done" button and understand Andy Grove's output equation for managers.

### 1.2 The "Empty Calendar" Panic  
Overcome the guilt of meetings and recognize that conversation *is* the work for managers.

### 1.3 Trust vs. Micromanagement (The 85% Rule)
Learn when to delegate and when to let go of perfection.

### 1.4 The Shit Umbrella vs. The Heat Shield
Master the art of filtering organizational chaos to protect your team without hiding reality.
